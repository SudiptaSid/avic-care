Our Service

We offer a wide range of services designed to provide safe, supportive and empowering living environments for individuals with mental health conditions and disabilities. Our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual, ensuring they have the care, independence and community support they deserve.

Accomodation

Supported Living Accommodation

We provide comfortable, fully equipped flats for individuals with mental health conditions and disabilities. Each flat is designed to promote independence while ensuring safety and accessibility. Our homes offer:

  • Adapted living spaces to meet physical, cognitive, and sensory needs.
  • Privacy and independence, with the option of shared communal spaces for social interaction.
  • 24/7 staff support for those who need assistance with daily tasks or personal care.
Personalized Care Plans

Every individual is unique, and so are their care needs. Our team works closely with each individual, their family, and healthcare professionals to develop a tailored care plan that:

  • Addresses their personal goals and preferences.
  • Provides the right level of support to help them live as independently as possible.
  • Includes ongoing reviews to adapt to changing needs.
Mental Health Support

For individuals living with mental health conditions such as anxiety, depression, schizophrenia, or bipolar disorder, we offer:

  • One-on-one emotional and psychological support, including regular check-ins with care professionals.
  • Access to counselling and therapy services through partnerships with mental health providers.
  • Medication management and support in coordination with healthcare professionals.
Physical and Developmental Disability Care

We provide specialized care for individuals with physical, developmental or cognitive disabilities. Our services include:

  • Mobility assistance and access to mobility aids and adaptive equipment.
  • Personal care services, such as help with bathing, dressing and grooming.
  • Support for individuals with developmental disabilities, including autism, Down syndrome and learning disabilities, focusing on enhancing communication, daily living skills, and social interaction.
Life Skills and Independence Training

Our focus is on helping individuals develop and maintain the skills needed to live independently. We offer:

  • Life skills training, including cooking, budgeting, cleaning, and personal hygiene.
  • Social skills development to promote confidence in community settings.
  • Employment and education support, including job search assistance, resume writing, and connections to local programs and resources.
24/7 On-Site Support

Our dedicated team is available around the clock to ensure residents’ needs are met at all times. This includes:

  • Emergency assistance and immediate response to any issues or concerns.
  • Help with daily living tasks, such as meal preparation, medication reminders, or personal care.
  • Emotional support to assist residents in managing stress, anxiety, or other mental health challenges.
