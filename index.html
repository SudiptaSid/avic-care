We provide More than just a place to live.

Welcome to Avic Care

We provide comprehensive support and resources for individuals navigating mental health challenges, other diagnoses and disabilities. Our mission is to empower individuals and their families with the information, tools and support they need to live fulfilling lives, regardless of the challenges they face.

Our location in Bedford currently has occupancy for 27 Flats for individuals facing mental health challenges and other disabilities. We are committed to providing safe, supportive, and accessible living environments for individuals as we believe that everyone deserves a place to call home where they feel valued, empowered, and respected. Our aim is to offer more than just housing—we strive to create communities where individuals can thrive, receive the care they need, and live with dignity.

We Can Help You With

Personal care

We believe that every individual is unique, hence we take a collaborative approach to care.

Medication management

We ensure safe and timely medication management in-house to enhance service users’ health and well-being.

Meal planning and preparation

We support nutritious and personalised meal plans to support with service users’ health and dietary needs.

Budgeting and financial management

We also offer tailored budgeting and financial management services to ensure financial security.

Housekeeping and home maintenance

We have reliable housekeeping and home maintenance services to create a clean, safe, and comfortable living environment.

24/7 On-Site Support

Our experienced support staff is available around the clock, offering support with activities of daily living

Ensuring the safety and well-being of our individual is our highest priority

Vision

Our vision is a world where individuals with mental health conditions and other disabilities have access to the housing and support, they need to live independently. We strive to create housing solutions that empower individuals, reduce isolation and promote mental well-being and quality of life.

Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, inclusive housing solutions tailored to the unique needs of people living with mental health challenges and other disabilities. By offering safe, comfortable flats along with personalized care, we aim to foster independence while ensuring that individuals have the support they need to live fulfilling, meaningful lives.

Values

Our core values guide everything we do, from the care we provide to the communities we build. We are driven by a commitment to deliver compassionate, respectful, and empowering services that enhance the lives of individuals in need.

Who We Support

We support people living with one or more of the following conditions:

Acquired Brain injury

Challenging Behavior

Forensic History - S37/41

Dual Diagnosis

Mental Health Diagnosis

Autistic Syndrome Disorder

Learning Difficulties
